Selection Sorting

Selection sort is a simple sorting algorithm that sorts an array by repeatedly finding the minimum element from the unsorted part and putting it at the beginning.

Itteration-1

step-1

a[30, 50, 20, 10, 40]

First we will select the first a[0] element and comapre with all the other elements . If we found any smaller element than the first element then we will swap it.

Comparing a[0] with a[1]

30 < 50 (T) -->Swapping not required


step-2

a[30, 50, 20, 10, 40]

Comparing a[0] with a[2]

30 < 20 (F) -->Swapping required

a[20 ,50 , 30, 10, 40]


step-3

a[20, 50, 30, 10, 40]

Comparing a[0] that is 20 with a[3] that is 10

20 < 10 (F) -->Swapping required

a[10 ,50 , 30, 20, 40]


step-4

a[10, 50, 30, 20, 40]

Comparing a[0] that is 10 with a[4] that is 40

10 < 40 (F) -->Swapping not required

a[10 ,50 , 30, 20, 40]


Itteration-2

step-1

a[10, 50, 30, 20, 40]

Comparing a[1] that is 50 with a[2] that is 30

50 < 30 (F) -->Swapping required

a[10 ,30 , 50, 20, 40]


step-2

a[10, 30, 50, 20, 40]

Comparing a[1] that is 30 with a[3] that is 20

30 < 20 (F) -->Swapping required

a[10 ,20 , 50, 30, 40]


step-3

a[10, 20, 50, 30, 40]

Comparing a[1] that is 30 with a[4] that is 40

20 < 40 (T) -->Swapping not required

a[10 ,20 , 50, 30, 40]

So 10 and 20 is sorted
